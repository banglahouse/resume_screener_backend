Job Title: HR Recruiter – Non-Technical Roles

Location: Bangalore / Remote (India)

About the Role
We are looking for an HR Recruiter to manage end-to-end hiring for non-technical positions such as Sales, Marketing, Operations, Finance, and Customer Support. You will work closely with hiring managers to understand role requirements, source relevant candidates, and ensure a smooth candidate experience.

Key Responsibilities

Manage end-to-end recruitment lifecycle for non-technical roles (job posting to offer rollout)

Partner with hiring managers to understand job requirements and create compelling JDs

Source candidates through job portals, LinkedIn, references, and social media

Screen resumes, conduct telephonic/virtual HR rounds, and coordinate with interview panels

Schedule and track interviews and maintain accurate recruitment reports

Maintain and update candidate data in the ATS or Excel trackers

Communicate feedback to candidates and ensure a positive candidate experience

Support employer branding activities (career page content, social media posts, hiring campaigns)

Must-Have Requirements

2–4 years of experience in recruitment for non-technical roles

Experience hiring for positions like Sales Executive, Account Manager, Marketing Executive, Operations Executive, Customer Support, Finance Associate, etc.

Strong resume screening and candidate evaluation skills

Excellent communication and interpersonal skills

Ability to handle multiple open positions with tight timelines

Comfortable using job portals (Naukri, LinkedIn, Indeed, etc.) and maintaining Excel trackers

Nice to Have

Experience working with an Applicant Tracking System (ATS)

Prior experience in a startup or fast-paced environment

Basic understanding of HR policies and onboarding processes

Education

Bachelor’s degree in any discipline (MBA/PGDM in HR is a plus but not mandatory)
