Job Title: Sales / Account Manager – B2B SaaS

Location: Mumbai / Remote (India)

About the Role
We are looking for a Sales / Account Manager to own relationships with B2B clients, understand their requirements, and drive renewals and upsell opportunities. This is a non-technical, client-facing role focused on revenue growth and customer satisfaction.

Key Responsibilities

Manage a portfolio of existing B2B clients, acting as their primary point of contact

Understand client needs and align our product offerings to their business goals

Conduct regular review calls and presentations with key stakeholders

Drive renewals and identify upsell/cross-sell opportunities

Manage the complete sales cycle for assigned accounts: proposals, negotiations, and closures

Maintain accurate records in the CRM (e.g., HubSpot, Salesforce, or Excel)

Collaborate with internal teams (operations, product, finance) to ensure smooth delivery

Prepare monthly/quarterly sales reports and forecasts

Must-Have Requirements

3–5 years of experience in B2B sales or account management

Experience handling corporate clients (SaaS, services, recruitment, or related domains)

Strong communication, negotiation, and relationship-management skills

Ability to work with targets and revenue goals

Comfortable with basic tools like Excel, PowerPoint, and CRM systems

Nice to Have

Experience in SaaS or HR-tech / recruitment-tech domain

Prior experience working with international clients (US/EU/APAC)

Education

Bachelor’s degree in Business, Commerce, or any related field (MBA in Sales/Marketing is a plus)
